The Divine Principle

The Divine Principle is a transformative worldview written for a new age. It focuses on individual spiritual growth and development from a perspective rooted in God. The Divine Principle helps us to understand who we truly are and what we can do to reach the innate potential that God has always intended for us. The Divine Principle presents a unique story of God working through Biblical and world history. The purpose of which is to reawaken each person’ understanding of their divine contribution to bringing the world back into God’s family.
